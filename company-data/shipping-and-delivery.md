# Shipping & Delivery Policy

## Overview

We aim to provide a fast, reliable, and transparent delivery experience for all our customers. This document outlines our shipping timelines, charges, delivery methods, and important policies to help you understand how your order will be processed and delivered.

---

## Order Processing Time

- All orders are processed within **1–2 business days** after confirmation.
- Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed on the next business day.
- During peak seasons (sales, festivals), processing may take up to **3 business days**.

---

## Shipping Timelines

Estimated delivery timelines vary based on location:

### Within Metro Cities
- Delivery within **2–4 business days**

### Within Non-Metro Cities
- Delivery within **3–6 business days**

### Remote / Rural Areas
- Delivery within **5–8 business days**

---

## Same-Day & Express Delivery

- **Same-day delivery** is available in select cities for orders placed before 12 PM.
- **Express delivery (1–2 days)** is available at an additional cost.
- Availability depends on product stock and delivery location.

---

## Shipping Charges

| Order Value | Shipping Fee |
|------------|-------------|
| Above ₹999 | Free Shipping |
| Below ₹999 | ₹99 Flat Fee |
| Express Delivery | ₹199 – ₹299 |

- Shipping fees are calculated at checkout.
- Promotional offers may override standard shipping charges.

---

## Order Tracking

- Once your order is shipped, you will receive:
  - Tracking ID
  - Courier partner details
  - Tracking link via SMS/email

- Tracking updates may take **12–24 hours** to reflect.

---

## Delivery Partners

We work with trusted logistics partners to ensure timely delivery, including:
- Delhivery
- Blue Dart
- Ekart
- DTDC

---

## Delivery Attempts

- Our courier partners will attempt delivery **up to 3 times**.
- If all attempts fail, the package may be returned to our warehouse.
- You may be contacted via phone or SMS before delivery.

---

## Cash on Delivery (COD)

- COD is available for most locations.
- A small **COD handling fee (₹49)** may apply.
- Payment must be made in full at the time of delivery.

---

## Failed Delivery

Delivery may fail due to:
- Incorrect or incomplete address
- Recipient unavailable
- Phone not reachable

### What Happens Next?
- Courier will retry delivery
- If unsuccessful, order is returned
- You may need to place a new order

---

## Address Changes

- Address changes are allowed **only before shipment**.
- Once shipped, changes cannot be guaranteed.

---

## Delays & Exceptions

Delivery delays may occur due to:
- Weather conditions
- Natural disasters
- Strikes or lockdowns
- High order volumes during sales

We will keep you informed in case of significant delays.

---

## International Shipping

- Currently, we offer limited international shipping.
- Delivery timelines: **7–14 business days**
- Customs duties (if applicable) must be paid by the customer.

---

## Damaged or Open Package

If you receive a damaged or tampered package:

- Do NOT accept the delivery
- Take photos if possible
- Contact support immediately

---

## Split Shipments

- Your order may be shipped in multiple packages
- This happens if items are sourced from different warehouses
- No additional shipping fee will be charged

---

## Undeliverable Packages

Packages may be returned if:
- Address is incorrect
- Delivery attempts fail
- Customer refuses delivery

Returned orders may require re-shipping charges.
