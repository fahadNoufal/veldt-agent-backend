# Return & Refund Policy

## Overview

We want you to love what you wear. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a simple and transparent return and refund process. Please read the policy carefully to understand eligibility, timelines, and conditions.

---

## Return Eligibility

You may request a return if:

- The item is unused, unwashed, and in its original condition
- All tags, labels, and packaging are intact
- The return request is initiated within **7 days of delivery**
- The item is not marked as **non-returnable**

---

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return:

- Products marked as **Final Sale**
- Discounted items above 50% off
- Innerwear and intimate products
- Customized or altered items
- Gift cards or promotional items

---

## Return Window

- Returns must be initiated within **7 days** from the date of delivery
- After 7 days, return requests will not be accepted

---

## How to Initiate a Return

1. Go to your order history
2. Select the item you want to return
3. Choose a reason for return
4. Submit the request

Alternatively, you can contact customer support for assistance.

---

## Return Pickup

- Once approved, a pickup will be scheduled within **1–3 business days**
- The item must be packed securely
- Ensure original packaging is included

If pickup is not available in your area:
- You may need to self-ship the item
- Shipping charges may be reimbursed upon approval

---

## Refund Process

### Refund Timeline

- Refunds are processed within **5–7 business days** after the item passes quality check
- The amount will be credited to your original payment method

### Refund Modes

| Payment Method        | Refund Method           |
|----------------------|------------------------|
| Credit/Debit Card    | Original card          |
| UPI                  | Same UPI account       |
| Net Banking          | Bank account           |
| Cash on Delivery     | Bank transfer / store credit |

---

## Exchange Policy

We currently offer exchanges only for:

- Size issues
- Defective products

### Exchange Conditions

- Subject to product availability
- Same product only (no style change)
- Initiate within 7 days of delivery

---

## Quality Check (QC)

- All returned items go through a quality inspection
- Refunds/exchanges are approved only if:
  - Item is unused
  - No damage caused by customer
  - Tags are intact

If QC fails:
- Item will be returned to you
- Refund will not be issued

---

## Damaged / Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

- Report within **48 hours of delivery**
- Share images/videos as proof
- We will prioritize replacement or full refund

---

## Missing or Incorrect Items

If your order has missing or incorrect items:

- Contact support within **48 hours**
- Provide order details and proof
- We will resolve it promptly

---

## Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received your refund:

1. Check your bank account
2. Contact your bank/payment provider
3. Wait for processing time (up to 7 days)

If still unresolved, contact support.

---

## Return Shipping Charges

- Free return pickup for eligible orders
- Self-ship returns may be reimbursed (based on approval)
- Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to an error from our side

---

## Cancellation Policy

### Before Shipment
- Orders can be cancelled for a full refund

### After Shipment
- Cancellation not allowed
- You can initiate a return after delivery

---

## Store Credit (Optional)

In some cases, refunds may be issued as **store credit**:

- Faster processing
- No bank delays
- Valid for future purchases

---

## Fraud Prevention

We reserve the right to:

- Reject returns that do not meet policy conditions
- Block accounts with excessive or suspicious return behavior
