# Payment & Checkout Information

## Overview

We offer a secure and seamless checkout experience with multiple payment options to suit your convenience. This guide explains accepted payment methods, checkout flow, common issues, and important policies.

---

## Accepted Payment Methods

We support the following payment options:

### 1. Credit / Debit Cards
- Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, American Express
- Secure payments via trusted payment gateways
- OTP verification required for most transactions

### 2. UPI (Unified Payments Interface)
- Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM
- Instant and secure transactions
- No additional charges

### 3. Net Banking
- Available for major banks
- Redirected to your bank’s secure login page

### 4. Cash on Delivery (COD)
- Available in most serviceable locations
- Pay at the time of delivery
- Additional COD fee may apply

### 5. Wallets (if applicable)
- Paytm Wallet, Amazon Pay, etc.
- Quick checkout using stored balance

---

## Checkout Process

Follow these steps to complete your order:

1. Add products to your cart
2. Review your cart items
3. Click on **Proceed to Checkout**
4. Enter or select delivery address
5. Choose payment method
6. Confirm and place your order

---

## Order Confirmation

- After successful payment, you will receive:
  - Order confirmation message
  - Order ID
  - Payment confirmation
- Confirmation is sent via SMS/email

---

## Payment Security

- All transactions are encrypted using **SSL (Secure Socket Layer)**
- We do not store your card details
- Payments are processed through trusted third-party gateways

---

## Payment Failures

A payment may fail due to:

- Incorrect card/UPI details
- Insufficient balance
- Network issues
- Bank server downtime

### What to Do?

- Retry using the same or a different payment method
- Check your bank account for deductions
- If amount is deducted but order not confirmed:
  - It will be refunded automatically within **5–7 business days**

---

## Double Payment / Duplicate Charges

If you are charged twice:

- One charge will be automatically reversed
- If not, contact support with transaction details

---

## Cash on Delivery (COD) Details

- Available for eligible pin codes
- Maximum order value limit may apply
- Exact amount should be paid at delivery
- Partial payments are not allowed

---

## Partial Payments

- Not supported currently
- Full payment must be completed to confirm the order

---

## Order Cancellation & Payment

### Before Shipment
- Full refund will be issued to original payment method

### After Shipment
- Cancellation not allowed
- You can initiate a return after delivery

---

## Refunds for Paid Orders

- Refunds are processed to the original payment method
- Timeline: **5–7 business days after approval**
